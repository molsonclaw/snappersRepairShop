About Snappers Repair

For over 25 years, Snappers Repair Shop has been the trusted name in automotive repair in Fountain City, Wisconsin and the surrounding Tri-State area.

We work on all makes and models - cars, trucks, SUVs, and vans. From routine oil changes to major engine work, our experienced technicians get it done right the first time.

We believe in honest work at fair prices. No upselling, no unnecessary repairs. Just straight talk and quality service that keeps you on the road.

  • Locally owned and operated
  • Experienced, certified technicians
  • Fair, transparent pricing
  • All makes and models welcome
